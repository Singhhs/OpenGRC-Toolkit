### 2.2 Rating Definitions

**Finding Severity:**

| Rating | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Critical** | Fundamental control weakness requiring immediate action. Significant risk to operations, compliance, or security. |
| **High** | Significant control weakness. Prompt remediation required. Material risk if not addressed. |
| **Medium** | Control weakness that should be addressed. Moderate risk that requires attention. |
| **Low** | Minor control weakness or opportunity for improvement. Low risk. |

**Overall Assessment Rating:**

| Rating | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Satisfactory** | Controls are adequate and operating effectively. Minor improvements may be needed. |
| **Needs Improvement** | Control weaknesses exist that require attention. Some controls are inadequate or not operating effectively. |
| **Unsatisfactory** | Significant control weaknesses. Major remediation required. Unacceptable level of risk. |
