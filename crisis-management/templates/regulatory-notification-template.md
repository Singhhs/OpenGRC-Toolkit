# Regulatory Notification Templates

Templates for notifying regulatory authorities about security incidents and data breaches.

**IMPORTANT:** All regulatory notifications must be reviewed by Legal before submission.

## Notification Timing Reference

| Authority | Regulation | Deadline |
|-----------|------------|----------|
| EU Data Protection Authorities | GDPR Art. 33 | 72 hours from awareness |
| US State Attorneys General | Various state laws | Varies (typically 30-90 days) |
| HHS/OCR (HIPAA) | HIPAA Breach Rule | 60 days (within 60 days of discovery) |
| SEC (Public Companies) | Item 1.05 of Form 8-K | 4 business days of materiality determination |
| PCI SSC | PCI DSS | Immediately to card brands |
| FTC | FTC Act | If required by settlement/order |
