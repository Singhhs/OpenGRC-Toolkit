## Usage Guidelines

### Before Using Templates

1. Select the appropriate template for your audience and situation
2. Replace all placeholder text `[IN BRACKETS]`
3. Remove sections that don't apply to your situation
4. **Review with Legal/Communications before sending externally**
5. Keep copies of all communications sent

### Approval Requirements

| Template Type | Approval Required |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Internal Alert | Security/IT Leadership |
| Executive Briefing | CISO |
| Employee Communication | CISO + HR |
| Customer Notification | Legal + Communications + Executive |
| Regulatory Notification | Legal |
| Press Statement | Legal + Communications + CEO |
| Partner Notification | Legal + Business Owner |

## Communication Best Practices

### Do's
- Be clear, factual, and concise
- Show empathy and take responsibility
- Provide actionable guidance
- Include contact information for questions
- Update stakeholders regularly

### Don'ts
- Speculate about cause or blame
- Minimize the incident
- Make promises you can't keep
- Delay notification unnecessarily
- Forget to coordinate timing of notifications
