# Employee Communication Templates

Templates for communicating with employees during security incidents.

## Initial Employee Alert

### Email Template - Incident Awareness

**To:** All Employees

**From:** [CEO / CISO / HR]

**Subject:** Important: Security Incident - Please Read

---

Team,

I'm writing to inform you of a security incident that [our security team has detected / we are currently investigating].

**What We Know**

[Brief, appropriate description of what happened without technical details that could compromise the investigation]

**What We're Doing**

- Our security team is actively investigating
- We have engaged [external cybersecurity experts / law enforcement] as appropriate
- We are taking all necessary steps to [contain / resolve] this situation

**What You Need to Do**

1. **Be vigilant** - Watch for suspicious emails, calls, or requests
2. **Report anything unusual** - Contact IT Security at [EMAIL/PHONE]
3. **Do not discuss externally** - Please keep this information confidential
4. [Additional specific instructions if needed]

**What You Should NOT Do**

- Do not discuss this incident on social media
- Do not speak to media - direct all inquiries to [COMMUNICATIONS CONTACT]
- Do not speculate about what happened

We will provide updates as we learn more. Thank you for your cooperation and patience.

[NAME]
[TITLE]

## Employee Data Affected Notification

### Email Template - When Employee Personal Data Compromised

**To:** [AFFECTED EMPLOYEES or ALL EMPLOYEES]

**From:** [CEO / CISO / HR]

**Subject:** Important: Your Personal Information May Have Been Affected

---

Dear [NAME / Team],

We are writing to inform you that a recent security incident may have affected some of your personal information.

**What Happened**

On [DATE], we discovered [brief description]. Our investigation indicates that employee information [was / may have been] accessed.

**What Information Was Affected**

The following types of your information may have been involved:
- [List specific data types: Name, SSN, address, salary, bank details, etc.]

**What We Are Doing**

- Completed forensic investigation with [SECURITY FIRM]
- Enhanced security controls to prevent similar incidents
- [Notified appropriate authorities]

**What We Are Providing**

To help protect you, we are offering:

- **[X] months of credit monitoring** through [PROVIDER]
  - Enrollment instructions: [DETAILS]
  - Enrollment deadline: [DATE]
  - Enrollment code: [CODE]

- **Identity theft protection services** including:
  - [Service 1]
  - [Service 2]

**What You Can Do**

1. **Enroll in credit monitoring** using the information above
2. **Monitor your accounts** for any unauthorized activity
3. **Consider a credit freeze** if concerned about identity theft
4. **Be alert for phishing** attempts that may reference this incident

**Resources**

- HR Contact: [NAME] at [EMAIL/PHONE]
- IT Security: [EMAIL/PHONE]
- Credit Monitoring Enrollment: [URL]
- FTC Identity Theft Resources: identitytheft.gov

**Questions?**

We have scheduled information sessions:
- [DATE/TIME] - [LOCATION/LINK]
- [DATE/TIME] - [LOCATION/LINK]

You may also contact [HR CONTACT] directly with any questions or concerns.

We sincerely apologize for this incident and are committed to supporting you through this process.

[NAME]

## All-Hands Meeting Talking Points

### For In-Person/Virtual Employee Update

**Opening**
- Thank everyone for attending
- Acknowledge the situation seriously
- Commit to transparency

**What Happened**
- Facts only, appropriate level of detail
- Timeline of events
- How it was discovered

**What We're Doing**
- Response actions taken
- External support engaged
- Regulatory notifications (if applicable)

**Impact on Employees**
- What employee data (if any) was affected
- What services are being provided
- How to access support

**What Employees Should Do**
- Specific, actionable guidance
- What to report and how
- What to avoid (media, social media, speculation)

**Q&A Guidelines**
- Prepared answers for anticipated questions
- "We don't know yet" is acceptable
- Redirect legal/media questions appropriately

**Closing**
- Reiterate commitment to security
- Provide contact information
- Announce next update timing
