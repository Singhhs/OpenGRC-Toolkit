## Severity Quick Reference

| Level | Criteria | Response |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **P1 Critical** | Active attack, data exfiltration, business-threatening | All hands, executive notification |
| **P2 High** | Significant impact, contained threat | Priority response, management notification |
| **P3 Medium** | Limited impact, localized incident | Standard response |
| **P4 Low** | Minimal impact, routine security event | Normal process |
