# Customer Notification Template

Use this template to notify customers about security incidents that may affect them.

**IMPORTANT:** All external communications must be reviewed by Legal and Communications before sending.

---

## Customer Email Notification

### Initial Notification

**Subject:** Important Security Notice from [ORGANIZATION NAME]

---

Dear [CUSTOMER NAME / Valued Customer],

We are writing to inform you of a security incident that may affect your information.

**What Happened**

On [DATE], we discovered [brief, clear description of what happened]. Upon discovery, we immediately [key actions taken - e.g., "launched an investigation," "engaged cybersecurity experts," "secured our systems"].

**What Information Was Involved**

Based on our investigation, the following types of information may have been affected:

- [Information type 1 - e.g., Name]
- [Information type 2 - e.g., Email address]
- [Information type 3 - e.g., Account number]

[If applicable: Your payment card information / Social Security number was NOT affected by this incident.]

**What We Are Doing**

We take the security of your information seriously. In response to this incident, we have:

- [Action 1 - e.g., Engaged leading cybersecurity experts to investigate]
- [Action 2 - e.g., Enhanced our security monitoring]
- [Action 3 - e.g., Notified law enforcement]
- [Action 4 - e.g., Implemented additional security measures]

**What You Can Do**

We recommend you take the following precautions:

1. **Monitor your accounts** - Review your account statements and credit reports for any unauthorized activity
2. **Change your password** - Update your password for your [ORGANIZATION NAME] account and any other accounts where you use the same password
3. **Enable two-factor authentication** - Add an extra layer of security to your account
4. **Be alert for phishing** - Be cautious of emails, calls, or messages asking for personal information

[If offering credit monitoring:]
As an additional precaution, we are offering you [X months/years] of complimentary credit monitoring and identity protection services through [PROVIDER NAME]. To enroll, please visit [URL] and use enrollment code: [CODE]. You must enroll by [DATE].

**For More Information**

We have established a dedicated response line to answer your questions:

- **Phone:** [TOLL-FREE NUMBER] (Available [HOURS])
- **Email:** [DEDICATED EMAIL ADDRESS]
- **Website:** [URL TO FAQ PAGE]

We sincerely regret any concern or inconvenience this incident may cause you. Protecting your information is our priority, and we are committed to preventing incidents like this from occurring in the future.

Sincerely,

[NAME]
[TITLE]
[ORGANIZATION NAME]

## Notification Checklist

Before sending customer notifications:

- [ ] Legal review completed
- [ ] Communications/PR review completed
- [ ] Executive approval obtained
- [ ] Notification timing coordinated (regulatory deadlines met)
- [ ] Call center briefed and staffed
- [ ] FAQ page published
- [ ] Credit monitoring vendor ready (if applicable)
- [ ] Email/letter distribution list verified
- [ ] Backup communication channels ready
- [ ] Media statement prepared (if needed)

## Regulatory Timing Requirements

| Regulation | Requirement | Timeline |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| GDPR | Notify affected individuals | Without undue delay |
| CCPA | Notify California residents | Without unreasonable delay |
| HIPAA | Notify affected individuals | Within 60 days |
| State Laws | Varies by state | Typically 30-90 days |
| Contractual | Per contract terms | Per contract |

**Note:** Consult with Legal to confirm specific notification requirements.
