## 2. Common Criteria (CC) - Security

*Required for all SOC 2 engagements*

### CC1 - Control Environment

#### CC1.1 - Integrity and Ethical Values

**Criteria:** The entity demonstrates a commitment to integrity and ethical values.

**Points of Focus:**
- Sets the tone at the top
- Establishes standards of conduct
- Evaluates adherence to standards
- Addresses deviations in a timely manner

**Common Controls:**
| Control | Evidence |
|---------|----------|
| Code of conduct policy | Policy document |
| Ethics training | Training records |
| Ethics hotline/reporting | Hotline records |
| Disciplinary procedures | Procedure documentation |

---

#### CC1.2 - Board Oversight

**Criteria:** The board of directors demonstrates independence from management and exercises oversight.

**Points of Focus:**
- Establishes oversight responsibilities
- Applies relevant expertise
- Operates independently
- Provides oversight for the system of internal control

**Common Controls:**
| Control | Evidence |
|---------|----------|
| Board/audit committee charter | Charter document |
| Board meeting minutes | Meeting minutes |
| Board composition documentation | Org chart, bios |
| Independence requirements | Policy documentation |

#### CC1.3 - Organizational Structure

**Criteria:** Management establishes structures, reporting lines, and appropriate authorities and responsibilities.

**Points of Focus:**
- Considers all structures

**Common Controls:**
