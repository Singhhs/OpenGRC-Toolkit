## 1. Purpose

This procedure defines the process for conducting management reviews of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:
- All scheduled management reviews
- All ISMS elements and processes
- All members of the management review committee
- All departments within the ISMS scope

## 3. Frequency

| Review Type | Frequency | Participants |
|-------------|-----------|--------------|
| Full management review | At least annually | Full committee |
| Interim review | Quarterly (recommended) | Core committee |
| Ad-hoc review | As needed | As appropriate |

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### 4.1 Top Management
- Attend and participate in reviews
- Make decisions on ISMS changes
- Allocate resources as needed
- Demonstrate commitment to ISMS

### 4.2 ISMS Manager / CISO
- Schedule and organize reviews
- Prepare review inputs
- Present ISMS status
- Document outcomes
- Follow up on actions

### 4.3 Department Managers
- Provide departmental input
- Report on security matters
- Implement assigned actions

## 5. Procedure

### 5.1 Pre-Review Preparation (2-4 weeks before)

**Step 1: Schedule the Review**
- Set date, time, and location
- Send calendar invitations
- Confirm attendee availability

**Step 2: Gather Review Inputs**

Collect and prepare information on:

| Input Category | Source | Responsible |
|----------------|--------|-------------|
| Status of previous actions | Action log | ISMS Manager |
| Changes in external/internal issues | Risk register, business changes | All managers |
| Information security performance | Metrics, dashboards | ISMS Manager |
| Nonconformities and corrective actions | Audit reports, NCR log | Quality/ISMS Manager |
| Monitoring and measurement results | KPIs, reports | ISMS Manager |
| Audit results | Internal audit reports | Audit Manager |
| Fulfillment of objectives | Objectives tracker | ISMS Manager |
| Feedback from interested parties | Complaints, suggestions | Various |
| Risk assessment results | Risk register | Risk Manager |
| Opportunities for improvement | All sources | All managers |

**Step 3: Prepare Agenda and Materials**
- Create meeting agenda (see [Management Review Agenda Template](../../../templates/management-review-agenda.md))
- Compile supporting documents
- Distribute materials at least 1 week before

### 5.2 Conducting the Review

**Step 4: Open the Meeting**
- Confirm quorum (minimum attendees)
- Review and approve previous minutes
- Confirm agenda

**Step 5: Review Each Input Item**

For each agenda item:
1. Present the current status/data
2. Discuss implications
3. Identify any required actions
4. Document decisions

**Step 6: Required Discussion Topics (per ISO 27001 9.3)**

| Topic | Questions to Address |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Previous actions** | Are all actions from last review complete? |
| **External/internal changes** | What changes affect the ISMS? |
| **Performance feedback** | Are we meeting security objectives? |
| **Nonconformities** | What issues have arisen? Are they resolved? |
| **Audit results** | What did audits find? Are findings addressed? |
| **Objectives status** | Are security objectives being met? |
| **Interested party feedback** | What feedback have we received? |
| **Risk assessment results** | How has risk profile changed? |
| **Improvement opportunities** | Where can we improve? |

**Step 7: Determine Outputs**

The review must result in decisions on:
- Opportunities for improvement
- Need for changes to the ISMS
- Resource needs

### 5.3 Post-Review Activities

**Step 8: Document the Review**

Minutes must include:
- Date, time, location
- Attendees
- Items reviewed
- Decisions made
- Actions assigned (with owners and due dates)
- Next review date

**Step 9: Distribute Minutes**
- Finalize within 1 week of meeting
- Distribute to all attendees
- File in ISMS records

**Step 10: Track Actions**
- Enter actions into action tracking system
- Assign owners and due dates
- Monitor progress
- Report status at next review

## 6. Review Inputs Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure all required inputs are gathered:

- [ ] Status of actions from previous management reviews
- [ ] Changes in external and internal issues relevant to ISMS
- [ ] Feedback on information security performance including:
  - [ ] Nonconformities and corrective actions
  - [ ] Monitoring and measurement results
  - [ ] Audit results
  - [ ] Fulfillment of information security objectives
- [ ] Feedback from interested parties
- [ ] Results of risk assessment and status of risk treatment plan
- [ ] Opportunities for continual improvement

## 7. Review Outputs Checklist

Ensure the review addresses:

- [ ] Decisions related to continual improvement opportunities
- [ ] Any needs for changes to the ISMS
- [ ] Resource needs identified and addressed
- [ ] Actions assigned with owners and deadlines
- [ ] Next review scheduled

Maintain the following records:

| Record | Retention | Location |
|--------|-----------|----------|

| Attendance records | Minimum 3 years | [Document repository] |
| Supporting materials | Minimum 3 years | [Document repository] |
| Action log | Active + 3 years | [Tracking system] |

## 9. Key Performance Indicators

Track the following:
- Management review completion rate (target: 100%)
- Action completion rate (target: >90%)
- Average days to complete actions
- Attendance rate (target: >80%)
