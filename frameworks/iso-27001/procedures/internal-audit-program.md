# Internal Audit Program

## 1. Purpose
To verify that the ISMS conforms to its own requirements and ISO 27001:2022, and is effectively implemented and maintained.

## 2. Roles
- **Audit Manager:** Plans and manages the audit schedule.
- **Internal Auditor:** Conducts the audit (must be independent of the area being audited).

## 3. Audit Schedule
Audits are conducted at planned intervals.

| Audit Area | Frequency | Planned Month | Auditor |
|------------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Context & Leadership (Cl. 4, 5) | Annually | January | External/Internal |
| Risk & Support (Cl. 6, 7) | Annually | April | External/Internal |
| Operations (Cl. 8) | Bi-Annually | July | External/Internal |
| Perf. Eval & Improv. (Cl. 9, 10) | Annually | October | External/Internal |
| Annex A Controls | Rolling 2-year cycle | Ongoing | External/Internal |

## 4. Audit Process
### 4.1. Preparation
- Define audit criteria and scope.
- Review previous audit reports.

### 4.2. Execution
- Conduct interviews, observe processes, and review documents/records.
- Collect objective evidence.

### 4.3. Reporting
- Document non-conformities (NCs) and opportunities for improvement (OFIs).
- Prepare an Audit Report.

### 4.4. Follow-up
- Management of the audited area must implement corrective actions for any NCs.
- Verification of corrective actions is performed in subsequent audits.
