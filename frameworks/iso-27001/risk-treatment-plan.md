# Risk Treatment Plan

## 1. Introduction
This Risk Treatment Plan (RTP) outlines the actions selected to address the risks identified during the risk assessment process for [Organization Name].

## 2. Risk Treatment Options
Risks are treated using one of the following options:
- **Modify (Mitigate):** Apply controls to reduce probability or impact.
- **Retain (Accept):** Accept the risk if it falls within the risk appetite.
- **Avoid:** Cease the activity causing the risk.
- **Share (Transfer):** Insure or outsource the risk (e.g., cyber insurance).
