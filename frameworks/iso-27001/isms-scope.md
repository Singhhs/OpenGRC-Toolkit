# ISMS Scope

## 1. Introduction
This document defines the scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) for [Organization Name], in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

## 2. Organizational Context
[Organization Name] provides [Description of Services/Products]. The internal and external issues relevant to our purpose and strategic direction have been determined in the Context of Organization document.

## 3. Scope Statement
The scope of the ISMS includes:
- **Products/Services:** All development, operations, and support of the [Product Name] platform.
- **Locations:** The primary office at [Address] and remote work environments.
- **Assets:** All information assets, including people, processes, and technology supporting the above services.

## 4. Exclusions
There are no exclusions from the requirements of ISO/IEC 27001:2022. All controls in Annex A are considered for applicability in the Statement of Applicability (SoA).

## 5. Interfaces and Dependencies
The ISMS relies on the following key interfaces:
- **Cloud Service Providers:** AWS/Azure/GCP for infrastructure hosting.
- **Third-Party Vendors:** For HR, payroll, and collaboration tools.

## 6. Review and Approval
This scope is reviewed at least annually or upon significant changes to the organization's business operations.
