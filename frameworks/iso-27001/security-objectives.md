# Information Security Objectives

## 1. Introduction
This document establishes the information security objectives for [Organization Name] for the year [Year], aligned with the Information Security Policy and business goals.

## 2. Setting Objectives
Objectives are established annually by key stakeholders and are:
- Consistent with the Information Security Policy.
- Measurable (where practicable).
- Applicable to information security requirements and results of risk assessment.
- Monitored, communicated, and updated.

## 3. Strategic Objectives
| ID | Objective | Target | Measure | Responsible |
|----|-----------|--------|---------|-------------|
| OB-01 | Maintain ISO 27001 Verification | 0 Major Non-conformities | External Audit Report | CISO |
| OB-02 | Ensure Staff Awareness | 100% Completion of Training | Training Logs | HR / Sec Team |
| OB-03 | Minimize Incident Response Time | < 4 hour Initial Response | Incident Tickets | Incident Resp. Lead |
| OB-04 | Critical System Availability | 99.9% Uptime | Monitoring Dashboard | Ops Lead |

## 4. Monitoring and Reporting
Progress toward these objectives is reviewed quarterly during the Management Review meetings.

## 5. Planning to Achieve Objectives
For each objective, the organization determines:
- What will be done.
- What resources will be required.
- Who will be responsible.
- When it will be completed.
- How the results will be evaluated.
