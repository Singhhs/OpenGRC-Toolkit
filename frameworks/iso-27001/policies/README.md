## Required vs. Recommended

### Mandatory (ISO 27001 Clause 5.2)

The standard explicitly requires:
- **Information Security Policy** - Top-level policy approved by management
- Must include commitment to satisfy requirements
- Must include commitment to continual improvement

### Critical for Certification

These policies address key control areas auditors will examine:
- Access Control Policy
- Incident Response Policy
- Business Continuity Policy
- Human Resources Security Policy
- Asset Management Policy

### Supporting Policies

Additional policies that facilitate implementation and demonstrate comprehensive coverage of Annex A controls.
