# Risk Assessment Methodologies

Guidance on conducting information security risk assessments.

## Available Methodologies

| Methodology | Use Case |
|-------------|----------|
| [General Risk Assessment](./risk-assessment-methodology.md) | Standard organizational risk assessment |
| [Vendor Risk Assessment](./vendor-risk-methodology.md) | Third-party risk evaluation |

## Methodology Comparison

| Aspect | ISO 27005 | NIST SP 800-30 | FAIR |
|--------|-----------|----------------|------|
| **Approach** | Qualitative | Qualitative | Quantitative |
| **Best For** | ISO 27001 compliance | US federal, NIST frameworks | Financial quantification |
| **Complexity** | Medium | Medium | High |
| **Output** | Risk levels | Risk levels | Financial estimates |
