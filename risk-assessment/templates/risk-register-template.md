## Risk Categories

| Category | Description | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Cybersecurity** | Threats to information systems | Malware, hacking, DDoS |
| **Operational** | Business process risks | System failure, human error |
| **Compliance** | Regulatory and legal risks | GDPR violations, audit findings |
| **Third Party** | Vendor and partner risks | Supplier breach, service failure |
| **Physical** | Environmental and physical risks | Fire, theft, natural disaster |
| **Strategic** | Business and market risks | Competition, market changes |
