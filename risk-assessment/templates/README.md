# Risk Assessment Templates

Ready-to-use templates for risk assessment activities.

## Available Templates

| Template | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [Risk Register](./risk-register-template.md) | Track and manage all identified risks |
| [Risk Assessment Report](./risk-assessment-template.md) | Document risk assessment findings |
| [Risk Treatment Plan](./risk-treatment-plan-template.md) | Plan and track risk treatments |
| [Asset Inventory](./asset-inventory-template.md) | Catalog information assets for assessment |
| [Threat Catalog](./threat-catalog.md) | Reference list of common threats |

## How to Use

1. Download the appropriate template
2. Replace placeholders with your organization's information
3. Customize scales and criteria to match your risk appetite
4. Use consistently across assessments
