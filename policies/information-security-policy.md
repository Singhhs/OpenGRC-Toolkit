## 1. Purpose

This Information Security Policy establishes the framework for protecting [ORGANIZATION NAME]'s information assets. It defines the principles, responsibilities, and requirements for maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third parties with access to [ORGANIZATION NAME] information systems
- All information assets owned, leased, or managed by [ORGANIZATION NAME]
- All locations where [ORGANIZATION NAME] business is conducted

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Information Security Principles

[ORGANIZATION NAME] is committed to:

1. **Protecting information assets** from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction
2. **Ensuring compliance** with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations
3. **Maintaining business continuity** through appropriate security measures
4. **Promoting security awareness** among all personnel
5. **Continuously improving** the information security management system

### 3.2 Information Security Objectives

The organization shall:

- Maintain an Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned with ISO 27001
- Conduct regular risk assessments and implement appropriate controls
- Ensure security incidents are reported, investigated, and resolved
- Provide adequate resources for information security
- Monitor and measure security performance

<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Add specific organizational objectives -->

### 3.3 Risk Management

[ORGANIZATION NAME] shall:

- Identify and assess information security risks regularly
- Implement controls proportionate to the assessed risks
- Accept residual risks only with appropriate management approval
- Document and maintain a risk register
- Review and update risk assessments at least annually

### 3.4 Compliance

The organization shall:

- Identify applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements
- Implement controls to ensure compliance
- Regularly verify compliance through audits and assessments
- Address non-compliance promptly and effectively

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### 4.1 Executive Management
- Provide strategic direction for information security
- Approve the information security policy and major decisions
- Allocate adequate resources
- Demonstrate leadership and commitment

### 4.2 [CISO / Information Security Manager]
- Develop and maintain the ISMS
- Report on security performance to executive management
- Coordinate security initiatives across the organization
- Manage security incidents and investigations

### 4.3 [IT Department / IT Manager]
- Implement and maintain technical security controls
- Monitor systems for security events
- Manage access controls and user accounts
- Maintain security of infrastructure

### 4.4 Department Managers
- Ensure staff compliance with security policies
- Identify and report security risks in their areas
- Participate in risk assessments
- Support security awareness initiatives

### 4.5 All Personnel
- Comply with all security policies and procedures
- Report security incidents and vulnerabilities
- Complete required security training
- Protect information assets in their care

## 6. Policy Compliance

### 6.1 Compliance Monitoring
Compliance with this policy will be monitored through:
- Regular internal audits
- Security assessments and reviews
- Incident analysis
- Management reviews

### 6.2 Exceptions
Exceptions to this policy require:
- Documented business justification
- Risk assessment of the exception
- Approval from [CISO / Security Manager]
- Defined duration and review date

### 6.3 Violations
Violations of this policy may result in:
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Legal action where appropriate
- Revocation of access privileges

## 7. Policy Review

This policy shall be:
- Reviewed at least annually
- Updated when significant changes occur
- Approved by executive management after each revision
- Communicated to all relevant parties
