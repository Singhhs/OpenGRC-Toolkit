## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes information security requirements for the employment lifecycle at [ORGANIZATION NAME], from pre-employment through termination, to reduce security risks from personnel.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees (permanent, temporary, part-time)
- Contractors and consultants
- Third-party personnel with access to company systems
- All stages of employment relationship

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Pre-Employment

#### 3.1.1 Background Verification

Before granting access to information assets:

| Check Type | Required For | Notes |
|------------|--------------|-------|
| Identity verification | All personnel | Government-issued ID |
| Employment history | All personnel | Last [X] years |
| Education verification | Where qualifications required | Highest relevant degree |
| Professional certifications | Where required for role | Verify validity |
| Criminal background | Roles with sensitive access | Per applicable law |
| Credit check | Financial/fiduciary roles | Per applicable law |
| Reference checks | All personnel | Minimum [2] references |

<!-- CUSTOMIZE: Adjust based on local laws and regulations -->

**Requirements:**
- Verification completed before start date or access granted
- Results documented and retained securely
- Adverse findings reviewed by HR and hiring manager
- Local legal requirements followed

#### 3.1.2 Job Descriptions

Job descriptions shall include:
- Information security responsibilities
- Required security clearances or background checks
- Confidentiality requirements
- Acceptable use acknowledgments required

#### 3.1.3 Terms of Employment

Employment contracts/agreements shall include:
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure obligations
- Intellectual property assignment
- Acceptable use acknowledgment requirement
- Security policy compliance requirement
- Consequences of policy violations
- Post-employment obligations

### 3.2 During Employment

#### 3.2.1 Management Responsibilities

Managers shall:
- Ensure staff understand security responsibilities
- Ensure compliance with security policies
- Identify and report security concerns
- Support security training participation
- Model appropriate security behavior
- Manage access based on job requirements

#### 3.2.2 Security Awareness and Training

| Training Type | Audience | Frequency |
|---------------|----------|-----------|
| General security awareness | All personnel | On joining + annual |
| Role-specific security | Personnel in specific roles | On assignment + annual |
| Privileged access training | IT administrators | On assignment + annual |
| Data protection/privacy | All personnel | On joining + annual |
| Phishing awareness | All personnel | Quarterly simulations |
| New threat awareness | All personnel | As needed |

**Requirements:**
- Training completion tracked
- Competency verified
- Non-completion escalated
- Training records retained

#### 3.2.3 Confidentiality Agreements

| Agreement Type | Required For | Timing |
|----------------|--------------|--------|
| Employee NDA | All employees | Before access granted |
| Contractor NDA | All contractors | Before engagement |
| Third-party NDA | External parties with access | Before access granted |

Agreements shall cover:
- Definition of confidential information
- Obligations during relationship
- Obligations after termination
- Consequences of breach

#### 3.2.4 Performance and Disciplinary Process

**Security-Related Performance:**
- Security responsibilities included in performance reviews
- Security incidents considered in reviews
- Positive security behavior recognized

**Disciplinary Process:**

| Violation Severity | Examples | Action |
|--------------------|----------|--------|
| Minor | First-time policy violation, accidental | Warning, retraining |
| Moderate | Repeated violations, negligence | Written warning, access restriction |
| Serious | Intentional violation, data breach | Suspension, termination |
| Gross | Fraud, theft, sabotage | Immediate termination, legal action |

**Requirements:**
- Fair and consistent process
- Investigation before action
- Documentation maintained
- Legal compliance ensured

#### 3.2.5 Role Changes

When employees change roles:
- Access reviewed within [5] business days
- Unnecessary access removed
- New access approved per procedures
- Training provided for new responsibilities
- Knowledge transfer completed

### 3.3 Termination and Change

#### 3.3.1 Termination Process

**Voluntary Termination:**
- Standard notice period served
- Access reviewed during notice
- Enhanced monitoring if required
- Exit interview conducted
- Assets returned on last day
- Access revoked on last day

**Involuntary Termination:**
- Access disabled before or during notification
- Physical access removed
- Escorted exit if appropriate
- Assets recovered immediately
- Legal obligations communicated

**Contract Completion:**
- Access revoked on contract end date
- Assets returned
- Knowledge transfer completed
- Final deliverables received

#### 3.3.2 Return of Assets

All company assets must be returned including:
- Laptops and computers
- Mobile devices
- Access cards and keys
- Documents and files
- ID badges
- Company credit cards
- Intellectual property

#### 3.3.3 Access Revocation

| Termination Type | Access Revocation Timing |
|------------------|--------------------------|
| Voluntary (standard) | End of last working day |
| Voluntary (sensitive role) | Immediately or during notice |
| Involuntary | Before notification |
| Contractor end | Contract end date |
| Death | Immediately upon notification |

Systems to disable:
- Network/VPN access
- Email
- All applications
- Physical access
- Remote access
- Cloud services

#### 3.3.4 Exit Interview

Exit interviews should cover:
- Reminder of ongoing confidentiality obligations
- Return of all assets and information
- Forwarding/transition of responsibilities
- Removal of personal data from company systems
- Agreement acknowledgment

#### 3.3.5 Post-Employment Obligations

Former personnel must:
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Not use proprietary information at new employer
- Honor non-compete agreements (if applicable)
- Not solicit employees (if applicable)
- Return any company property discovered later

### 3.4 Contractors and Third Parties

#### 3.4.1 Requirements

- Background checks appropriate to access level
- Confidentiality agreements signed
- Security policies acknowledged
- Sponsoring employee assigned
- Access limited to job requirements
- Access time-limited
- Regular access review

#### 3.4.2 Contractor Management

- Contracts include security requirements
- Performance monitored
- Access reviewed quarterly
- Incidents reported and managed
- Termination process defined

## 4. Special Situations

### 4.1 Extended Leave

For extended leave (>30 days):
- Access suspended during leave
- Reactivation upon return with verification
- Assets may be returned during leave
- Alternative contact arrangements made

### 4.2 Internal Investigation

During security investigations:
- Access may be suspended pending investigation
- Suspension not indicative of guilt
- Fair investigation conducted
- Employee rights respected
- Legal consultation as appropriate

### 4.3 Insider Threat Indicators

Be alert to:
- Unusual working hours
- Accessing data beyond job scope
- Expressed grievances
- Financial difficulties
- Changes in behavior
- Policy violations

Report concerns to [Security/HR].

## 5. Roles and Responsibilities

### 5.1 Human Resources
- Conduct background verification
- Manage employment contracts
- Coordinate termination process
- Maintain training records
- Manage disciplinary process

### 5.2 IT/IT Security
- Provision and revoke access
- Support background verification systems
- Monitor for security concerns
- Manage security training systems

### 5.3 Managers
- Ensure team compliance
- Participate in hiring process
- Report security concerns
- Support termination process
- Complete access reviews

### 5.4 All Personnel
- Comply with security policies
- Complete required training
- Report security concerns
- Protect company information
- Return assets when required

## 6. Compliance

### 6.1 Monitoring

- Background check completion tracking
- Training completion monitoring
- Termination process audits
- Policy compliance reviews

### 6.2 Legal Compliance

This policy complies with:
- Employment laws
- Privacy laws
- Background check regulations
- Data protection requirements
